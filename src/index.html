Agourelaio

Unripe Olives.
Maximum Potency.

Farmako Agourelaio is harvested early — when the olives are still green and unripe. This ancient Greek practice, known as agourelaio, yields an oil far richer in polyphenols and vitamins than conventional extra virgin olive oil.

10–15ml daily. In salads, soups, as a finishing oil — or simply as a supplement, as generations of Greek families have done.

🫒 "We want you to take 10–15ml every day.
As a multi-vitamin. As a ritual."
